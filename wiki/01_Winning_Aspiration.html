1. Winning Aspiration

The Guiding Star: To define the purpose of our enterprise in an era where "intelligence" is a commodity.

The Current State

We are "The best place for the best people." We are rooted in the Nordics but hungry for the world. We aim to be the preferred global transformation partner.

The Ambition:

  • Growth: Grow at 20% YoY.
  • AI Penetration: 20% of revenue comes directly from AI services, and 100% of consultants use AI for delivery.
  • Integration: AI is not just a vertical; it is infused into all services.

The Strategic Beliefs (Our Answers)

1. The "Get Shit Done" Premium

If Implement Consulting Group disappeared tomorrow, what would the world miss?

The Answer: The world would miss the human bridge between "Messy Reality" and "Perfect Strategy."

  • Insight: AI is flawed, messy, and theoretical. Our clients value our "get shit done" attitude—the ability to navigate the political and emotional messiness of implementation that AI cannot touch.
  • Strategic Choice: We do not sell "Intelligence" (which is becoming a commodity); we sell "Execution Power". We are the hands that guide the AI, not just the brain that prompts it.

2. The Resilience of Culture

Can we scale "intimacy" and "autonomy" globally without losing our soul?

The Answer: Yes. We have grown before without breaking, and AI will not break us either.

  • Insight: The fear of "growing too big" is a constant ghost, but our culture has proven resilient.
  • Strategic Choice: We will use AI to protect our culture, not dilute it. We will automate the bureaucracy so we can spend more time on the "human" interactions that define us. We will not compromise our DNA for efficiency.

3. The "Loneliness" Defense

In 2030, why would a brilliant human join us instead of starting their own AI-powered studio?

The Answer: Because Consulting is a Team Sport, and the Network is the Moat.

  • Insight:
    1. The B2B Barrier: Selling to enterprises requires trust and a network that a solo AI-studio cannot easily replicate.
    2. The Social Animal: A solo studio is lonely. Humans crave connection. We offer the "Tribe"—a global network of brilliant minds to spar with.
    3. The Level Playing Field: If AI makes the individual super-intelligent, it makes the client super-intelligent too. The differentiator remains the Brand, the Network, and the Human Connection.
  • Strategic Choice: We position Implement not just as a "workplace" but as a "High-Performance Community" that you cannot access alone.

Emerging Hypotheses

  • Hypothesis A: "The Global Tribe". Our winning aspiration is to be the world's most connected community of transformation experts, where AI handles the data, but humans handle the relationships.
  • Hypothesis B: "The Execution Engine". We aspire to be the only firm that guarantees results, not just advice, because we master the "messy middle" where AI fails.
