6. Strategic Questions & Trigger Points

The Watchtower: A living list of the "Big Questions" we haven't answered yet, and the "Trigger Points" that will force us to act.

Trigger Points

If the world changes X, we do Y.

  • Trigger 1: If AI commoditizes "Strategy Formulation" (i.e., GPT-6 writes better strategies than partners)...
    • Then: We shift 100% of our business model to "Execution & Change". We stop selling "slides" and sell "outcomes."
  • Trigger 2: If Clients successfully build internal "AI Studios"...
    • Then: We pivot from "Building AI for them" to "Governing their AI". We become the "Quality Assurance" and "Ethics" auditors for their internal teams.
  • Trigger 3: If A major competitor (McKinsey/Accenture) releases a fully automated "Consultant-in-a-Box"...
    • Then: We double down on "High-Touch" and "Political Complexity". We market ourselves as the premium human alternative to their robot.

The Deep Dive: Questions Behind the Question

These are the fundamental tensions that underpin our strategic choices. We must solve these to unlock the answers.

  1. M&A: The Culture vs. Speed Paradox

    • If we buy to speed up (Acqui-hire), do we dilute the 'Implement Soul' that is our main differentiator?

  2. Product: The Service vs. Product Tension

    • Can a consultancy sell 'products' without becoming a vendor? Does the 'product' serve the 'advice', or does the advice sell the product?

  3. Internal Apps: The Builder's Trap

    • Does building our own OS make us 'sovereign' or just 'distracted'? Are we building for client value or consultant ego?

  4. Hiring: The Generalist's Fate

    • In a world of specialized AI agents, is the 'Smart Generalist' a dying breed or the only breed that matters?

  5. Commercial: The Value Abyss

    • If we stop charging for time, we must charge for value. But are we brave enough to share the risk of that value not materializing?
