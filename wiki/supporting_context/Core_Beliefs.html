Core Beliefs

The philosophical convictions that drive our strategy. These are not market predictions (Assumptions), but moral and existential stances.

1. Purpose is Deeply Human

"AI can do anything, but it can't tell us what to do."

  • The Belief: In a world of infinite execution capacity, Intent becomes the scarcest resource.
  • The Insight: AI stares back at you and asks, "What is next?" It requires a prompt.
  • The Role: The future "Trusted Advisor" is not the one who knows the most (AI does that), but the one who helps the client figure out what is worth doing. We are the architects of Purpose, not just the managers of Process.

2. The "Best Place" is a Community

"A solo studio might be powerful, but you will be lonely."

  • The Belief: Humans are social animals who crave connection, friction, and belonging.
  • The Insight: Even if a "Super-Consultant" with AI can do the work of 10, they will still choose to join Implement because of the Tribe. The "Network" is not just a sales channel; it's an emotional necessity.

3. We Work for Everyone

  • The Belief: Transformation is universal.
  • The Reality: We serve the "Legacy Giants" (Novo Nordisk) and the "Small Businesses" alike. Our sweet spot is the complex organization (1,000 - 20,000 employees) that is too big to turn easily but too small to be bureaucratic zombies.
  • The Ambition: We want to work with the biggest, but we will never lose our ability to help the smallest.
